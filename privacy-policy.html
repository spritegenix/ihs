Effective Date: December 06, 2024

At *Ideal Healthcare Services, we are committed to protecting your personal information in compliance with the **UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR), the **Data Protection Act 2018*, and other relevant UK data protection laws. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

*Personal Details:* Name, address, phone number, email address, date of birth, and identification documents (e.g., passport or driving license).
*Professional Details:* CV, qualifications, employment history, references, and training records.
*Financial Information:* Bank details for payroll purposes.
*Compliance Data:* Criminal records (DBS checks), right-to-work documentation, and health declarations.
*Technical Data:* IP address, browser type, and other website usage data.


2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data to:

*Recruitment Services:* Match candidates with job opportunities based on their qualifications and preferences.
*Compliance:* Ensure legal and regulatory compliance, such as DBS checks and right-to-work requirements.
*Payroll Processing:* Manage payroll and expenses
*Communication:* Send updates, respond to inquiries, and provide essential information.
*Training and Development:* Offer training courses and track certifications.
*Analytics:* Improve our services and user experience.


3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data under the following legal bases:

*Consent:* When you explicitly agree to us processing your data.
*Contractual Necessity:* To fulfill contractual obligations with you or your employer.
*Legal Obligations:* To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.
*Legitimate Interests:* To provide efficient recruitment services and manage our business operations.


4. Data Sharing

4.1. We may share your information with:

Potential employers, including NHS Trusts and private healthcare providers.
Regulatory authorities, such as the Disclosure and Barring Service (DBS).
Payroll and training providers.
IT service providers for system maintenance and support.
We ensure all third parties comply with UK data protection laws and only process your data under our instructions.


5. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law. Typically, this is:

*Recruitment Data:* 6 years after the end of your relationship with us.
*Financial Data:* 7 years for tax and accounting purposes.

6. Your Rights

Under the UK GDPR, you have the following rights:

*Access:* Request access to your personal data.
*Rectification:* Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data.
*Erasure:* Request deletion of your data, subject to legal obligations.
*Restriction:* Restrict processing of your data in certain circumstances.
*Portability:* Request your data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
*Objection:* Object to data processing based on legitimate interests or direct marketing.
To exercise your rights, contact us at recruitment@ideal-healthcare.co.uk


7. Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. These include:

Encryption of sensitive data.
Access control and authentication measures.
Regular audits and updates to security protocols.


8. Cookies and Website Use

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience and analyze traffic. For more details, refer to our *Cookie Policy*.


9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on our website, and significant updates will be communicated directly to you where possible.
